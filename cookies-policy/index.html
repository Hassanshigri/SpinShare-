What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, smartphone, or tablet when you visit a website. They contain data that helps websites remember information about your visit—such as your preferred settings or login state. Cookies are a common and essential part of modern web functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use a limited range of cookie types strictly necessary to operate Spinnshare as a secure and user-friendly platform:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform features like login authentication, game session management, and age verification.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us track how users interact with the site, such as which games are most played or which pages load slowly. This information is used to improve performance and user experience.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language settings, theme preferences, or other customized features across sessions.
We do not use advertising or marketing cookies. Your data is never sold, and we do not track you across third-party sites.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies on Spinnshare allow us to:

  • Improve page load times and detect performance issues
  • Remember your interaction preferences
None of the cookies we use collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment data, or real identity details. All data is anonymous or pseudonymized for your privacy and security.

Managing and Controlling Cookies

When you first visit Spinnshare, you will see a cookie popup allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies. Your choice will be saved using local storage on your device to avoid repeated prompts.

You can also manage or delete cookies manually through your browser settings. Each browser has different options for controlling cookies, such as clearing them entirely or limiting them to specific sites. Please note that disabling essential cookies may limit core functionality, such as login access or game progress tracking.
